Electronic Technician

Aros Electronics AB

📍 Mölndal, Sweden 🗓️ December 2025 - April 2026 · Timed Contract

As an Electronic Technician at Aros Electronics AB, I supported high-volume production lines for PCB assembly by maintaining and troubleshooting automated assembly equipment. My role focused on ensuring stable machine uptime and consistent product quality by performing preventive maintenance, rapid fault diagnosis, and careful calibration of production systems.

I regularly worked hands-on with electronics by soldering and reworking defective boards, performing functional testing, and debugging electrical circuits to identify root causes of failures. This involved verifying assemblies against documentation, using measurement equipment, and collaborating closely with operators and production staff to reduce recurring defects and improve overall throughput.

In addition to technical maintenance and repair, I handled warehouse and storage management for a broad inventory of electronic components, ensuring accurate organization, traceability, and availability for production. I also configured manufacturing parameters and assembly line settings for different PCB products, supporting smooth changeovers and reliable production runs.

Key Responsibilities & Skills

Maintenance & Troubleshooting

  • Preventive and corrective maintenance on PCB assembly production equipment
  • Fault isolation and root-cause analysis for recurring machine and process issues
  • Setup and tuning of manufacturing parameters and assembly line configurations
  • Production support during changeovers to ensure stable and repeatable output

Electronics, Testing & Rework

  • Soldering and rework of defective boards (SMT/through-hole as required)
  • Functional testing and debugging of electrical circuits and PCB assemblies
  • Reading schematics and following work instructions, BOMs, and test procedures
  • Safe handling of electronics including ESD awareness and quality-focused workmanship

Warehouse & Inventory Management

  • Organizing and maintaining a large inventory of electronic components and consumables
  • Component traceability, storage structure, and stock replenishment for production needs
  • Coordination with production to prioritize parts availability and minimize downtime
  • Attention to detail in labeling, handling, and storage to prevent mix-ups and defects
